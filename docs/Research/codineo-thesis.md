## Summary

The undergraduate thesis this site is built around. Submitted in March 2026 to the FEU
Institute of Technology in partial fulfilment of the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science
with specialization in Software Engineering. Adviser: Ms. Kim Giselle Bautista. Panel:
Dr. Hadji Tejuco (head), Ms. May Florence San Pablo, Dr. Dennis B. Gonzales.

## Why it matters for this thesis

It IS the thesis. Every other entry in `docs/Research/` is here because this paper cites or
relies on it. The structural choices in the C++ microservice (hash-based virtual structural
copy algorithm, simultaneous traversal-and-hashing, JSON-driven pattern catalog, AI
documentation layer) are all defined in Chapters 3 and 4 of this paper.

## What we use from it

- The system name **CodiNeo** and the route family `/` (this public site) are the public face
  of the paper's "C++ analysis studio + learning modules" subsystems.
- The reading-comprehension and design-pattern-learning framing on `/about` is sourced from the
  Significance of the Study (Chapter 1.2) and Statement of the Problem (Chapter 1.3).
- The five-stage pipeline on `/mechanics` is the public restatement of Algorithm + Project
  Design (Chapter 3.4 + 3.5).
- The supported design-pattern catalog (Singleton, Factory, Builder, Adapter, Strategy, plus
  C++20 idioms) is the operational scope defined in the Scope and Delimitations (Chapter 1.7.1).
- The DEVCON Luzon community context — interns, bootcamps, professional development projects —
  is the reason `/why` lists "Students" as a primary industry hook.
