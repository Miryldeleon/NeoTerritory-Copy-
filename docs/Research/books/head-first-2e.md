## Summary

Classic introductory text on design patterns, refreshed in 2020 to cover Java 8+ idioms. Conversational tone, heavy use of analogies and visual metaphors. Covers approximately 14 GoF patterns plus principles.

## Why it matters for this thesis

This is the book most undergraduates encounter first. Voice and pacing on `/learn` and `/student-studio` borrow from its tone (no jargon stacking, one new vocabulary item per paragraph). It is not the structural source for our pattern definitions — that role belongs to Dive Into Design Patterns — but it sets the readability bar for our beginner copy per D40.

## What we use from it

- Tone: conversational without being cute, technical without being dense.
- Pedagogical pacing: introduce concept, give one analogy, give one code example, then abstract.
- Pattern selection: Head First's smaller curated list is a hint about which patterns are worth shipping detectors for first.
